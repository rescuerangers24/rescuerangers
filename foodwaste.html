Reducing Food Waste

Why Food Waste Matters

Food waste is a significant issue affecting the environment and economy. When food is wasted, it not only represents a loss of resources but also contributes to greenhouse gas emissions as decomposing food releases methane. Reducing food waste helps conserve resources and lowers our carbon footprint.

Tips for Reducing Food Waste

Here are some practical tips for minimizing food waste:

  • Plan your meals and create a shopping list to avoid buying excess food.
  • Use proper food storage techniques to extend the shelf life of your groceries.
  • Repurpose leftovers into new meals to reduce waste.
  • Compost food scraps instead of throwing them away.
