Live

Clicking the "Live Updates" icon on our website opens a window that provides real-time information about our ongoing projects. This feature offers transparency by showcasing how donations are actively being used. Donors can see live streams of activities and updates, ensuring their contributions are making a tangible impact. The live feed includes detailed descriptions of the help being provided and the progress of various initiatives funded by donor generosity. By viewing these live updates, donors gain confidence in the effectiveness and integrity of our work. This transparency is crucial in building trust and maintaining accountability. The live updates are regularly refreshed, offering the latest information directly from our project sites. This interactive feature is designed to engage our community and show appreciation for their support. We believe in keeping our donors informed and involved every step of the way.
